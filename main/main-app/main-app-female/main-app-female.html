Ears are very important to this breed. Notice, they have to be well haired and should be clean at all times.

Eyes are large, round and also well apart; their colour should be deep and pure - blue, copper, orange or green, depending on the coat colour.

Paws should be massive and the nails may be cut once a while.

Tail should be around 15cm and rounded at the tip.
